Annie with an "E"

A plucky orphan whose passions run deep finds an unlikely home with a spinster and her soft-spoken bachelor brother. Based on "Anne of Green Gables." Emmy-winning writer-producer Moira Walley-Beckett ("Breaking Bad") puts a new twist on L.M. Montgomery's timeless tales.

Created by: Moira Walley-Beckett

Cast & Creative Team
